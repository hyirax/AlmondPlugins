### ✨ Introduction

**AlmondHousing** is an enhanced FFXIV housing plugin, deeply rebuilt and optimized from [DisPlace](https://github.com/Drakansoul/DisPlace), and independently maintained by **AlmondCookie**. It is dedicated to making complex housing decoration, material management, and data sharing more geeky, efficient, and simple.

**Core Features:**
* 🌍 **Seamless Multilingual Support**: Native support for English, Chinese (Simp/Trad), Japanese, Korean, German, and French.
* 📦 **Smart Material Audit**: Automatically scans your Inventory and Chocobo Saddlebags, compares them with the current layout, and calculates exactly what you are missing.
* 📊 **Advanced Export Options**: One-click export for **Teamcraft import strings** (with already-owned items filtered out) or detailed CSV shopping lists.
* 🏗️ **Layout Compatibility & Protection**: Full compatibility with `.json` layouts from MakePlace and ReMakePlace; supports the exclusive encrypted `.almond` format to prevent unauthorized tampering.

### 🚀 Installation

1. **Prerequisite**: You must use the [Dalamud / FFXIV Quick Launcher](https://goatcorp.github.io/) to run the game.
2. Type `/xlsettings` in the game chat to open Dalamud settings.
3. Navigate to the **"Experimental"** tab.
4. Add the following link to the **"Custom Plugin Repositories"** section:
   `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hyirax/AlmondHousing/master/pluginmaster.json`
5. Click the **+** icon, ensure **"Enabled"** is checked, and then click "Save and Close".
6. Search for **`AlmondHousing`** in the `/xlplugins` installer and click Install.

### 🎮 Quick Start Guide

Type **`/almond`** in the chat to open the control panel.

1. **Saving a Layout**: Enter a house where you have permissions, enable the built-in "Rotate Layout" mode, and click `Save As` to backup the current housing data.
2. **Preparing Materials**: Open the `Material Audit` tab to see which furniture items are missing and export the list for precise purchasing.
3. **Applying a Layout**: Place the required furniture in your inventory or onto the floor, then click `Select File & Apply`. The plugin will automatically position everything for you.

*(Note: DO NOT move your mouse or let your character block the furniture movement path during auto-placement to avoid game crashes.)*
