### ✨ Introduction

**AlmondHousing** is an FFXIV housing enhancement tool, born from the exploration and accumulation of community predecessors' projects, and independently maintained by **AlmondCookie**. The goal is to offer some additional convenience for players, within the boundaries of the game.

**Core Features:**
* 🌍 **Multilingual Support** — Native support for Chinese (Simp/Trad), English, Japanese, Korean, German, and French
* 📦 **Material Audit** — Automatically scans your inventory and chocobo saddlebag, compares against the current layout in real time, and accurately calculates what is missing
* 📊 **List Export** — One-click export to Teamcraft shopping lists or CSV spreadsheets
* 🔐 **Layout Compatibility** — Reads `.json` layouts from MakePlace and ReMakePlace, supports saving in `.almond` format

### 🚀 Installation

1. Launch the game using [Dalamud / FFXIV Quick Launcher](https://goatcorp.github.io/)
2. Type `/xlsettings` in chat to open Dalamud settings
3. Switch to the **"Experimental"** tab
4. Add the following to **"Custom Plugin Repositories"**:
   `https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hyirax/AlmondHousing/master/pluginmaster.json`
5. Click **+**, check **"Enabled"**, then save and close
6. Search for **AlmondHousing** in `/xlplugins` to install

---

### 📋 Quick Start

Type **`/almond`** in chat to open the panel.

1. **Save Layout** — Enter a house where you have permissions, enable housing rotation mode, and click `Save As` to back up
2. **Prepare Materials** — Switch to the `Material Audit` tab to check missing items, export the list for precise purchasing
3. **Apply Layout** — Place required furniture in your inventory or on the floor, then click `Select File & Apply` for automatic placement

*(During auto-placement, please avoid moving the mouse or blocking furniture movement paths with your character)*
